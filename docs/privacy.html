1. Introduction

Welcome to Tazakar ("we", "our", or "the app"). This Privacy Policy explains what information Tazakar collects, how it is used, and the choices you have regarding your data.

Tazakar is designed from the ground up as a privacy-first application. Our fundamental principle is simple: your personal data stays on your device. We do not operate a server that stores your reminders, voice recordings, or any personally identifiable information.

By installing and using Tazakar, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Stored Locally On Your Device Only

All of the following data is created and stored exclusively on your device, encrypted with AES-256, and never transmitted to us or any third party:

  • Reminder content — the text, dates, times, and categories of reminders you create
  • Voice utterances — audio captured when you use voice input is processed entirely on-device and is never recorded, stored as audio, or transmitted anywhere
  • App preferences — your language and dialect preferences, theme selection, notification settings, and other in-app settings
  • Notification history — a local log of when reminders were fired, snoozed, or completed, used solely to improve notification reliability
Your voice data is never recorded. Tazakar activates the microphone only when you initiate voice input. Audio is processed in real time by on-device AI and immediately discarded. No audio file is ever saved or transmitted.

2.2 Anonymous Technical Data (Crashlytics)

To improve app stability, Tazakar uses Firebase Crashlytics (a free Google service). If the app crashes, the following anonymous technical information is transmitted to Google:

Data TransmittedPurposePersonal?
Stack trace (code error location)Identify and fix bugsNo

What is never transmitted in crash reports: your name, reminder content, voice recordings, location, contact information, device identifier, IP address, or any user-generated content.

2.3 Firebase Remote Configuration

Tazakar uses Firebase Remote Config (a free Google service) solely to read a single app configuration flag (MONETIZATION_ACTIVE) on each app launch. This fetch transmits no user data — only a standard anonymous HTTPS request is made to retrieve the configuration value.

2.4 In-App Purchases (Pro Subscription)

If you purchase a Tazakar Pro subscription, the payment transaction is processed entirely by Apple App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android). Tazakar does not handle, process, or store your payment details. Your subscription status is stored locally on your device in encrypted storage. We do not create or maintain a Tazakar account for you — your Apple ID or Google Account manages your purchase identity.

4. Data Storage & Security

All reminder data, preferences, and app content stored on your device is protected with AES-256-CBC encryption at the database level. The encryption key is generated on your device during first launch and stored exclusively in the iOS Keychain (iPhone/iPad) or Android Keystore (Android devices) — hardware-backed secure enclaves that no app, including Tazakar, can read the key from directly.

This means that even if someone obtained physical access to your device storage, your reminder data would be unreadable without the encryption key held in your device's secure enclave.

Data never leaves your device for AI processing, storage, or synchronisation. There is no Tazakar cloud database. There is no Tazakar server that holds your reminders.

6. Your Rights & Controls

Delete All Your Data

You can delete all data stored by Tazakar at any time through Settings → Delete All Data. This action permanently and irreversibly destroys your encrypted database and all encryption keys. It cannot be undone.

Export Your Data

You can export all your reminder data as a JSON file at any time through Settings → Export Data. This export is shared via your device's standard sharing options (AirDrop, Files, email, etc.) and remains on your device — it is not transmitted to us.

Uninstalling the App

Uninstalling Tazakar removes all app data from your device. There is no server-side account to delete because we do not hold your data on any server.

Opt Out of Crash Reporting

If you wish to disable anonymous crash reporting, you may do so via Settings → Privacy → Crash Reporting. Disabling this does not affect any core app functionality.

7. Children's Privacy

Tazakar is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or under 16 in certain jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. As Tazakar does not collect personal data from any user, this risk is inherently minimal — but if you believe a child has used Tazakar in a way that raises privacy concerns, please contact us at the address below.

9. Legal Compliance

UAE Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL)

Tazakar is designed to comply with the UAE Federal Decree-Law No. 45 of 2021 on Personal Data Protection. As Tazakar does not collect, process, or transfer personal data to any external server, the compliance burden is inherently minimal. All data remains on the user's device under the user's sole control.

General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area, Tazakar's on-device-only data architecture means no personal data is transferred outside your device. Anonymous crash data transmitted via Firebase Crashlytics is governed by Google's data processing terms and their EU Standard Contractual Clauses.

Data Minimisation

We apply the principle of data minimisation throughout Tazakar's design: we collect only what is strictly necessary for the app to function, and we collect nothing that could identify you personally.
